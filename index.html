Loaded Mashed Potato Balls

Recipe by Amber Saleem

Ingredients

  • Cooked Chicken1/2 cup(diced)
  • Leftover mashed Potatoes3 cups
  • Shredded cheddar cheese1 cup
  • Black pepper powder1/2 tsp
  • Saltto taste
  • Eggs2 large(beaten)
  • Breadcrumbsas required
  • Oilfor deep fry

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl mix cooked chicken, mashed potato, cheddar cheese, black pepper and salt.
  2. Make small balls out of the mixture.
  3. Dip balls in egg then coat in breadcrumbs.
  4. Heat oil in a wok and deep fry balls in badges.
  5. Serve warm with tomato ketchup.
